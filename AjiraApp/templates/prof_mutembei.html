About Professor Mutembei

Prof. Henry Mutembei is an accomplished academic with decades of experience in university leadership, research development, and community engagement. His work spans academic reforms, infrastructure expansion, student welfare, and international partnerships that elevate the university's global competitiveness.

Leadership & Vision

Digital Transformation

Expansion of e-learning, digital libraries, and automated academic systems to enhance learning experiences.

Research Excellence

Promoting impactful, solution-driven research and postgraduate growth through strategic funding and support.

Infrastructure Growth

Leading major projects to modernize the university's teaching and research facilities for a better academic environment.

Student Welfare

Improving student services, accommodation, mentorship, and co-curricular development for holistic education.

Community Engagement

Strengthening partnerships with local communities and county governments for mutual development.

Global Partnerships

Establishing international MoUs to advance research, academic quality, and student exchange programs.

Key Achievements

  • 2023 Major infrastructure upgrades launched under his leadership, including new lecture halls and research facilities.
  • 2022 Strengthened international partnerships and MoUs with universities in Europe, Asia, and North America.
  • 2021 Introduced new academic programmes and research initiatives focused on emerging technologies and sustainable development.
  • 2020 Successfully navigated the university through the COVID-19 pandemic with minimal disruption to academic activities.
